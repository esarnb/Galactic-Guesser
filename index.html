Galactic Guesser

Welcome to Galactic Guesser! How to play:

  • Each planet as an assigned random value. (The first one, Pluto, will always be one.)
  • The goal is to click each planet, which raising your score, and matching it to the goal score.
  • You will be able to keep selecting planets to raise your score, but do not go over the goal limit!
  • Going over the goal limit will result in a loss! Try to figure out the shortest amount of clicks to reach the goal!
